Tile Africa
An experience redesign

Description

  • Client: Tile Africa
  • ECD: Simon Gill
  • Build: Donald Archer

The brief:
to perform a catalogue and marketplace makeover, refreshing Tile Africa's online presence and updating their customer experience. The aim was to improve interaction between customers and online users giving them the same level of inspiration they experience when they visit the stores in person.

The solution:
first a discovery and disruption session was held to ascertain the brand's strengths, weaknesses, potential opportunities and threats. The findings were then assessed and developed into a strategy.

The new-look and improved website included a responsive web and mobile site with an interactive gallery, a canvas tool, customer care interface with social media integration such as Pinterest, Facebook, and Twitter.
