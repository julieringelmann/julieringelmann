Disprin Cardiocare
Give your heart the help it needs

Description

  • Client: Reckitt Benckiser
  • ECD: Simon Gill

The brief:
To conceptualise a method to create easy-to-digest, shareable information for the purposes of educating South Africans on issues surrounding heart disease and prevention.

The solution:
After a series of wireframing and content revisions to meet with various compliance standards, responsive designs for an educational website with sharable content snippets were created to run in parallel with a social media campaign.

The use of information graphics was the natural simple solution which alleviated any need for users to spend time reading and understanding lots of text. The content and structure of information focussed on three main heart health categories: national and international statistics, what it is and how it works, and basic prevention and care.
