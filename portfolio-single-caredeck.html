Care Deck
Customer Service At Your Service

Description

  • Client: NXT\ Digital Innovation
  • ECD: Simon Gill

The brief:
Though Caredeck is a product of NXT\, it was decided that a rework of the logo and its accompanying CI was required to match the character of the Caredeck brand, a product that allows its users to master their customer relationships across multiple communication channels from one central dashboard.

The solution:
The brand refresh concept designs centred around recreating Caredeck's professional service delivery and attention to detail through the use of classic, minimalist black and white butler and service imagery paired together with the strategic use of spot a green and cherry-on-top red.

About Caredeck:
Caredeck (a proud venture owned and conceptualised by NXT\) is a tool that provides brands with key instruments to effectively identify, communicate and care for their customers whilst measuring their performance. Usage roles and viewing permissions ensure brands have full administrative control over the Caredeck features and functions while allowing their customers to start a meaningful real-time conversations via the channels of their choice.
